# **App Name**: MagicTea Elite

## Core Features:

- Fast-Touch POS Billing: High-speed, category-based billing interface optimized for touchscreens, featuring hot teas, cold beverages, and bakery items from 'The Magic Tea' menu.
- Recipe-Linked Stock Sync: Real-time engine that triggers automatic ingredient deduction (milk, tea powder, jaggery, packaging) from Firestore upon every finalized bill.
- Intelligent Inventory Forecast Tool: A tool that uses generative AI to analyze sales velocity and provide reasoning for stock replenishment schedules to prevent low-inventory scenarios.
- Daily Hisab Settlement: Comprehensive accounting ledger to reconcile opening cash, online sales, Zomato splits, and expenses with automated difference detection.
- Milk Utility & Wastage Tracker: Dedicated module for logging milk reception and granular consumption across tea production, cold shakes, and wastage for loss prevention.
- Staff Attendance & Payroll: PWA-enabled punch-in/out system with shift tracking, salary management, and employee advance records.
- Multi-Branch Management Hub: Scalable architecture allowing admin control across multiple shop locations with role-based access security for managers and staff.
- Vendor & Purchase Ledger: Comprehensive tracking of stock-in events, vendor details, purchase history, and gas/packing material inventory.
- Real-Time Analytical Dashboard: A centralized view of today’s sales, profit estimates, best-selling products, and low-stock alerts powered by real-time Firestore queries.
- Report Export Engine: On-demand generation of Daily, Monthly, and Product-Sales reports with PDF and Excel export functionality.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Brand Red (#D20103) for core identity and high-visibility action buttons.
- Secondary Color: Lustrous Gold (#D4AF37) for premium status badges, accents, and dividers.
- Background Color: Crisp White (#FFFFFF) to maintain a clean, high-contrast professional ERP aesthetic.
- Font Family: A mix of elegant Serif for branding headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for tabular data and POS items.
- Minimalist gold-contoured icons designed for rapid identification of tea, bakery, and stock categories.
- Mobile-first card-based architecture featuring sticky POS headers and quick-action bottom nav for branch staff.
- Fluid micro-interactions for billing confirmation and smooth slide-out drawers for stock adjustments.
